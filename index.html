Stephen King

Stephen Edwin King is an American author. Widely known for his horror novels, he has been crowned the "King of Horror".
He has written approximately 200 short stories. His debut, Carrie (1974), established him in horror. Among the films adapted from King's fiction are Carrie (1976), The Shining (1980), The Dead Zone and Christine (both 1983), Stand by Me (1986), Misery (1990), The Shawshank Redemption (1994), Dolores Claiborne (1995), The Green Mile (1999), The Mist (2007), It (2017) and Salem's Lot (2024).
He has published under the pseudonym Richard Bachman and has co-written works with other authors.
